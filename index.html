Perpetual Petals is a private floral membership serving a limited number of homes in Windermere, Florida.

Using premium garden roses, seasonal blooms, and delicate natural greenery, every design is composed to feel airy, romantic, and quietly luxurious. Arrangements are delivered, refreshed, and exchanged regularly — allowing flowers to become a living element of the space rather than a one-time detail.

Lori Fronzak

Founder & Floral Designer

Founded by Lori Fronzak (Ell), a female veteran whose background instilled a deep sense of discipline, integrity, and service. With a refined eye for composition and a deep appreciation for natural form, she creates airy, romantic arrangements that feel effortless yet intentional.
