Our Philosophy

Perpetual Petals is a private floral membership serving a limited number of homes in Windermere, Florida.

Using premium garden roses, seasonal blooms, and delicate natural greenery, every design is composed to feel airy, romantic, and quietly luxurious. Arrangements are delivered, refreshed, and exchanged on a regular cadence — allowing flowers to become a living element of your space rather than a fleeting detail.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the membership work?

Perpetual Petals is a private floral membership. Once approved, arrangements are designed and delivered on a recurring cadence — weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, or seasonally — based on your preference. Each placement is curated specifically for your home.

What flowers do you use?

We source premium garden roses, seasonal blooms, and delicate natural greenery from trusted growers. Every arrangement reflects what is freshest and most beautiful in the current season, ensuring a living, evolving presence in your space.

Can I choose my arrangements?

The design process is collaborative yet guided. During your initial consultation, Lori learns your aesthetic, your home, and your preferences. From there, each arrangement is composed intuitively — no two are ever alike.

How often are arrangements refreshed?

Frequency is entirely up to you. Most members choose weekly or bi-weekly placements to keep their flowers vibrant and alive. Seasonal and monthly options are also available for a more measured cadence.

Do you serve areas outside Windermere?

Our membership is centered in Windermere, Florida and the surrounding communities. Select areas beyond Windermere may be considered on a case-by-case basis — please inquire for availability.

How do I become a member?

Submit an application through our membership form. Availability is intentionally limited. Once received, we will reach out within 48 hours to schedule a complimentary consultation and discuss how Perpetual Petals can enhance your home.
