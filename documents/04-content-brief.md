## 1. Photography Requirements

High-quality imagery is the single most important content element for a luxury floral brand. Every photograph should communicate craftsmanship, exclusivity, and the natural beauty of the arrangements.

### 1.1 Arrangement Photography

- **Quantity:** 12--16 images
- **Minimum Resolution:** 2400px on the longest edge
- **Orientation:** A mix of landscape (for hero sections and banners) and portrait (for gallery and mobile display)
- **Subject Matter:**
  - Signature arrangements representing the range of styles offered
  - Seasonal variations if available (spring florals, winter textures, etc.)
  - Close-up detail shots highlighting texture, color gradients, and unique elements
  - At least 2--3 images showing scale (arrangement placed next to a recognizable object or in a room setting)

### 1.2 Lifestyle & Environment Shots

- **Quantity:** 3--5 images
- **Context:** Arrangements photographed in actual residential settings -- living rooms, dining tables, entryways, bedrooms
- **Goal:** Help prospective members envision the arrangements in their own homes
- **Notes:**
  - Homes should feel upscale but lived-in, not sterile or staged
  - Natural light strongly preferred
  - Avoid overly busy backgrounds; the arrangement should remain the clear focal point

### 1.3 Founder Portraits

- **Quantity:** 2--3 images
- **Style:** Natural light, candid preferred over formally posed
- **Context Suggestions:**
  - Working with flowers at the studio or workspace
  - Arranging or selecting stems
  - A relaxed, approachable headshot suitable for an "About" section
- **Notes:**
  - These portraits help build personal connection and trust with prospective members
  - Wardrobe should feel intentional but not overly formal -- aligned with the brand's refined-yet-approachable tone

### 1.4 Behind-the-Scenes & Process Images

- **Quantity:** 4--6 images
- **Subject Matter:**
  - Flower sourcing or selection (market visits, unpacking deliveries)
  - The arrangement process (hands working with stems, tools of the trade)
  - Packaging and preparation for delivery
  - Workspace or studio environment
- **Goal:** Communicate the artistry and care behind every arrangement; reinforce the handcrafted, small-batch nature of the membership

### 1.5 Technical Specifications

| Specification       | Requirement                                                  |
|---------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| Resolution          | Minimum 2400px wide; 4000px+ preferred for hero images       |
| File Format         | High-quality JPEG (.jpg) and/or lossless PNG (.png)          |
| Color Profile       | sRGB for web use                                             |
| Aspect Ratios       | Mix of 16:9 (banners), 4:3 (general), and 1:1 (social/grid) |
| Lighting            | Natural light preferred; soft, diffused artificial acceptable |
| Editing             | Light retouching only -- maintain natural color accuracy      |
| Backgrounds         | Clean, uncluttered; neutral tones or complementary settings   |
| File Size           | Uncompressed originals preferred; we will optimize for web    |

## 2. Recommended Photographer Brief

If you are working with a photographer (or if we are coordinating one on your behalf), the following guidance should be shared with them directly.

### 2.1 Shot List Summary

| Category                  | Qty     | Priority |
|---------------------------|---------|----------|
| Arrangement -- hero/wide  | 4--5    | High     |
| Arrangement -- detail     | 4--5    | High     |
| Arrangement -- scale      | 2--3    | Medium   |
| Lifestyle/in-home         | 3--5    | High     |
| Founder portraits         | 2--3    | High     |
| Behind-the-scenes         | 4--6    | Medium   |

### 2.2 Mood & Aesthetic References

- **Tone:** Warm, organic, elevated but not untouchable
- **Comparable Brands (for visual reference only):** Aesop, Le Labo, The Sill, Farmgirl Flowers
- **Color Palette Feel:** Muted earth tones, soft greens, warm whites, blush, terracotta -- avoiding harsh saturated colors
- **Avoid:** Overly dark or moody lighting, heavy vignetting, artificial-looking color grading, stock-photo aesthetics

### 2.3 Lighting & Composition Direction

- **Lighting:** Side-lit or backlit natural light is ideal. Golden hour and soft overcast conditions produce the best results. If shooting indoors, position near large windows. Avoid direct flash.
- **Composition:** Leave breathing room around subjects for text overlay in hero images. Use the rule of thirds. Include negative space intentionally -- the website design will utilize it.
- **Depth of Field:** Shallow depth of field for detail shots; moderate for lifestyle shots so the environment remains legible.
- **Angles:** Eye-level and slightly above for arrangements. Avoid extreme overhead (flat-lay) unless specifically for a detail or process shot.

## 3. Testimonial Collection

Authentic member testimonials are essential for building trust with prospective members. We need 3--5 polished quotes for use across the website, with the possibility of featuring additional quotes in future marketing materials.

### 3.1 What We Need

- **Quantity:** 3--5 member quotes
- **Length:** 2--4 sentences each (we can edit for brevity with approval)
- **Attribution:** First name and last initial, neighborhood or general location (e.g., "Sarah M., Windermere"), and membership tier if applicable
- **Photo (optional but encouraged):** A candid photo of the member with an arrangement, or a portrait for use alongside the quote

### 3.2 Member Questionnaire Template

The following questions are designed to elicit specific, genuine responses rather than generic praise. We recommend sending these via email or discussing them in person, then refining the responses into polished quotes for approval.

---

**Perpetual Petals -- Member Feedback**

Thank you for taking a few minutes to share your experience. Your words help others understand what makes this membership special.

1. What initially drew you to Perpetual Petals, and what made you decide to join?
2. How would you describe the experience of receiving your arrangements? Is there a specific delivery that stands out in your memory?
3. How have fresh arrangements changed the feeling of your home or a particular room?
4. What would you tell a friend who was considering a floral membership?
5. Is there a single word or phrase that comes to mind when you think of Perpetual Petals?
6. Is there anything about the service -- the quality, the personal attention, the consistency -- that has surprised you?

---

### 3.3 Guidance on Collecting Authentic Quotes

- Ask open-ended questions and let members respond in their own words before editing
- Specificity is more persuasive than superlatives -- "My dining room feels completely different on delivery day" is stronger than "It's amazing"
- Always obtain written permission before publishing any quote or likeness
- If a member is hesitant to write, offer to conduct a brief phone or in-person conversation and draft the quote for their review and approval

## 4. Copy Review

All website copy -- including homepage messaging, membership tier descriptions, the founder's story, FAQ content, and any supplementary page text -- will be provided to you for review before publication.

### 4.1 Review Process

1. We will deliver copy documents organized by page and section
2. Please review for factual accuracy, tone alignment, and any corrections or preferences
3. Mark any changes directly in the document or provide consolidated feedback
4. **Requested turnaround: 48 hours from delivery of each copy document**
5. One round of revisions is included; additional rounds can be accommodated within the project timeline

### 4.2 What to Look For

- Accuracy of all claims (membership details, pricing, service areas, delivery schedules)
- Tone -- does the language feel true to how you speak about your work?
- Any details you would like added, removed, or reframed
- Proper names, titles, and terminology

To keep files organized, please use the following naming structure where possible:

```

pp-lifestyle-dining-room-01.jpg
pp-founder-portrait-01.jpg
pp-bts-studio-workspace-01.jpg
pp-logo-primary.svg
```
